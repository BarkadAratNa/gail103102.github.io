BLARE'S BOARDS AND BISTRO

Have you ever uttered the phrase, “It’s so good to be back”? This place will surely let you reminisce about your childhood memories like playing board games with friends while eating or drinking. Blare’s Boards and Bistro is located at ground floor, Eco Commercial building, Brgy. Guinhawa, Malolos, Bulacan. In front of Bulacan State University. In this restaurant, you and your friends are not only enjoying mouthwatering food but you are also enjoying your childhood days because they have games that will surely let you go back to your old times. Not only board games but they also have a karaoke that you can avail when you reach the required to use the karaoke. It is good for celebrating birthdays or having a party. This restaurant has a stylish interior and a cozy atmosphere. In terms of food, they have an affordable and large portion that will satisfy your cravings. 

Address
