CROSSROADS

Do you want to hang out with your friends while drinking and listening to a live band? Well, this is the place that you must visit. Crossroads is located at Crossing near Chowking and 7 – 11. The place is an open food park/bar wherein it has a 3 story unit. It opens at 9 am up to midnight. On the first floor is the location of different food stations that will surely satisfy your cravings. Examples of food carts are Engilbert’s Silog, Macee Float, Ella’s House of Dimsum and Vita Bella. The second floor is the location of the performer and the drinking spot. This is an open space which means there is no aircon only electric fans. In terms of food, they have “pulutan” which a little bit pricey but quality over quantity isn’t? You can have fun as much as you want because even though there are other people they only care about their business. In terms of security, minors are not allowed because they might be sued for letting the minors drink. They are only allowed in the food park, on the first floor.
