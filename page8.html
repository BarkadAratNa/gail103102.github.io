KALYE POGI

Kalye Pogi is located in Capitol View Park Subd. Malolos Bulacan. This place is a drinking spot with KTV, they have a small room which cost 50 per hour while the Big room cost 100 per hour, it can accommodate 12 to 15 persons. They operate from 3 pm until 11 pm and vape inside is not allowed. In terms of food and drinks, their bucket costs 260 to 270 and it consists of 6pcs of beer. Their pulutan cost 100 to 160 which is affordable and taste good. Inside the KTV room is air - conditioned and cozy place.

Address

Malolos, Bulacan
