GARAJE FOOD PARK

Garaje is located in Isabel, Malolos Bulacan near Bulacan Sports Complex and Marcelo H. Del pilar High School. Garaje is a food park that has a budget - friendly meals for the students and the people working around the park. The food parks are Thriftea; it is a milk tea and frappe shop that is affordable and tasty. Next is Keritoh, they are offering french fries with hotdog or drinks. Blunch is also a food cart that offers siomai with rice. Another one is Wingsburg, their main dish is wings and they offer affordable meals. There are different varieties of meals and snacks that are not mentioned but its for you to find out. The place is not air -conditioned but the atmosphere is somehow cool because the place is surrounded by plants. The place is also Instagram worthy but it depends on your creativeness.
